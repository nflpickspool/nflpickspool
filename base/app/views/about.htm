Picks Pool Rules

Objective

To have the most points at the end of the season. More points = more cash.

There are 6 ways to earn points as detailed below.

The first three are bets made for each game.

The latter three are bets made with the entire season in mind.

Against the Spread (ATS)

You are required to pick every game against the spread. To earn points, the team you select must cover the spread.

You earn 1 point for a correct pick, 0 points for an incorrect pick, and 0 points for a push.

You may LOCK any game.

You earn 2 points for a correct lock, -1 points for an incorrect lock, and 0 points for a push.

Over/Under Totals

You may select whether a game’s total scored points will exceed (over) or fall below (under) the projected total.

You earn 1 point for a correct pick, -1 points for an incorrect pick, and 0 points for a push.

Money Line

Each game will have a money line number. To earn points, the team you select must win the game.

If you pick the favorite, you wager the money line to earn 1 point.

If you pick the underdog, you wager 1 point to earn the money line.

If a tie occurs (unlikely), it’s a “No Action” bet, 0 points are earned, and you get your wager back.

Suicide Pool

Pick one team each week that you think will win.

If that team wins, you move on to the following week. If they lose, you are eliminated.

You can only use each team once.

The player that makes it the furthest wins 5 points.

You can also earn 2 points for making it to week 5, and an additional point each week beyond week 5 that you survive.

Team Wins (Over/Under)

You are required to pick whether each team will win more than their projected win total for the season.

To earn points, the team must finish with more than that projection (over) or less than the projection (under).

A correct pick earns 2 points. An incorrect pick earns 0 points.

You may LOCK any team.

You earn 4 points for a correct lock, -2 points for an incorrect lock, and 0 points for a push.

Price

$250 per person for the season. 

Payouts are made to the top five players as follows:

1st Place: $XXX + (in dollars) the difference in points between themselves and 3rd place + the difference between themselves and 4th place + the difference between themselves and 5th place

2nd place: $XXX

3rd place: 50% of the remaining pot

4th place: 30% of the remaining pot

5th place: 20% of the remaining pot

Tie-Breakers

1-      Better ATS record (by %)

2-      Better Lock record (by %)

3-      Better Team Wins O/U record (by %)
